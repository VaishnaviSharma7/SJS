JIAGANJ,MURSHIDABAD

Principal’s Message

It gives me great pleasure to introduce you to the website of St. Joseph’s School, Jiaganj. It’s our earnest endeavor to reach out to all the concerned stakeholders through the rich online resources that provides all the necessary information about St. Joseph’s School, Jiaganj. It’s always a matter of curiosity to know what goes on inside a school by seeing the exterior façade of a school building.I am certain the relevant contents of this website will give everyone a glimpse of the innovative and creative way of teaching and the aspects of culture that school upholds.

Few years ago we never thought that true learning could happen out of a classroom, but today we have come to a time when teaching and learning happens from the homes of the student. Education has got this shift from lecture model to learning by doing. We at SJS have been trying our best to shift from mere rote learning to more engaging learning, which will enable students to attain the skills that are demanded today outside the school premises, which are problem solving, creativity and collaboration. We aim that Education must enable the marginalized, those who are treated by society a non-persons to regain their dignity and self worth.

We at SJS try to promote the values of equality, religious tolerance, love and empathy for the poor and needy, where equal importance is given to value education as well as respect for culture and traditions. Importance is also given to creating happy and healthy environment for the students so that learning becomes fun.

I am sure that this website will enable everyone to have an insight about the school and its activities.

Fr. Sanjay Mandal
Principal
